DOM4

This is just a fully tested and covered polyfill for new DOM Level 4 entries

  • Element#prepend()
  • Element#append()
  • Element#before()
  • Element#after()
  • Element#replace()
  • Element#remove()

The implemented test is conform to current specifications.

If you need other polyfills too have a look at DOM-shim repo.
